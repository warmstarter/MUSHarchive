Mystery Lake Map Notes

  • Link back to The Long Dark Mapping
  • Link back to Tips for The Long Dark Noobs

Mystery Lake: Namesake of the starting area is Mystery Lake, which has the Camp Office on the west side. Out on the ice are several fishing huts. Across the lake from the Camp Office are some abandoned cabins. A bear hangs out near one cabin.

I consider Mystery Lake the "center" of the map.

Southern River Access

  • The Southern River Access (leave the Office and go out onto the lake and then left) starts with the woods with the rabbit runs. This is a good place for snares.
  • Alan's Cave Blind: Keep going South/behind the bunnies is a Hunting Blind with some fixed supplies, as well as potentially a deer carcass (starter carcass). The blind also has rose hips and Old Man's Beard near it. There's a small alcove named Alan's Cave.
  • One outlet from the Alan's Cave Blind leads to the back side of the Carter Hydroelectric Dam (the side with the safe and the hallway down). If you walk up by the door but stay outside and then turn right and head into a channel between rocks it leads to a transition point called "Ravine". The Ravine leads to the Coastal Highway.
  • The other outlet from Alan's Cave Blind appears to loop around to the same area near the dam (following the frozen river at the base of the dam).

The Lake Path (west side of the Camp House)

There's no compass direction here, but this is the side of the setting sun. There's a length of train track with a lot of trees on the far side of the tracks.

  • North path of tracks. The north side leads to a Derailment. Keep heading north and you reach the Train Loading Area where can turn left for a logging road (yellow sign), keep going north, or turn right. Right loops back to Northern River Access path to Mystery Lake.
  • Logging Camp (logging road). The left turn onto the logging road leads to the Logging camp with the blue trailers. If the trailers are on the left and you're facing them, go right to find the Clearcut, or go left to head back towards Mystery Lake.
  • South path of tracks leads to a collapsed tunnel (opposite the side where you can see the cars). If you walk towards the collapsed tunnel but turn right to go down, through a fallen tree that looks like an arch, and then up the next hill, you reach the Trapper's Homestead. This cabin will have smoke filtering out of the chimney.
  • There's a nice little rabbit run near the Trapper's Homestead. Exit the Homestead and walk towards the rotting barn. Keeping the barn on your left, walk along a slight "S" twist around one rock outcropping, and then another. After the second rock outcropping is a hill with a lot of rabbits.
  • The hill with the rabbit run is the same hill that leads back to the train tracks near the Camp Office. To get to the Camp Office from the Trapper's Homestead, go up the hill with the rabbits, down the hill, and then up the next hill in line, passing under the fallen tree that looks like an arch.
  • Once you reach the Trapper's Homestead you can go to the hill behind it and continue on till you loop around to the deer blind at the Unnamed Pond off the Clearcut.

Clearcut

There are several access points off the Clearcut.

  • Logging Road Sign. There's a yellow logging road sign that leads to the Logging Camp with the blue trailers. The logging road path eventually leads to Mystery Lake.
  • Overlook Sign. There's a yellow and blue sign that marks the path to the Overlook. Take this path up to reach the Overlook.
  • If you stand in the Clearcut with the Overlook sign to your left, you can walk over the hill and find a small Cabin with an outhouse on the far side. There's a small rabbit run here but no interior fire. I would only use this as a Day One stop.
  • Keep walking away from the Clearcut and go past the small Cabin with the outhouse, and you'll reach the Unnamed Pond. It has a deer blind on the far side.

Western River Access

  • The western river splits into two sides of a fork. The left side goes to a dead end named "Dave's Quiet Retreat" with a corpse in it. The right side loops around to the Lake Trail which ends at the Camp Office.

Camp Office

Rabbit Runs:

  • Through the South River Access
  • Up towards the tracks and turn right. Look for the shiny blue ice/snow on the left side of the tracks. Go down and then up a small hill. Bunnies gather in the hollow on the other side of the hill.
  • You can also reach the Trapper's Homestead bunny run by going up to the tracks, turning left, and looking for the fallen tree.

Deer:

  • You can kill deer (or herd wolves into deer) both up along the train tracks behind the office, and down on the lake (especially in the stretch of ice between the cabin and the South River Access).

Fish:

  • There are fishing shacks all over Mystery Lake.

Firewood:

  • The Lake Path tends to have a lot of sticks.
  • You can get bigger branches by going across the lake and over to the Western River Access.
  • You can also find firewood scattered through the woods that run parallel to the train tracks behind the Office.
