DRYH Open Chargen

From Gwen Morse's Wiki
Jump to: navigation, search

Comments from Gwen: This is much more valuable than you might realize at first glance. All the difficult concepts behind statting, dice rolling, and designing a chargen are presented here in a simplified form. A coder still learning (or polishing) their skills can take this code and gradually build it up over time as they improve. But, in this form, it works from the moment of installation. Brilliant!

What makes it extra-brilliant? The chargen rooms (which scare most new wizards working on a chargen system). Actual stat systems are a dime a dozen...but the code for chargen rooms is often jealously guarded! 

@@ DRYH Stat, Dice, and Cgen rooms.
@@ Note: This code is for an open cgen with auto approvals and open +sheets. You will need to tweak the code if you wish to build in a hold for review before approving. Cgen will create a +job to let you know that a character has finished cgen.
@@ Creates the DRYH +sheet commands
@@ Note: '+sheet name' is usuable by anyone. Ie: Any player object can see the +sheet of any other player object in the game. This should be easily lockable if staff does not want open +sheets.
@pemit me=Creating the +sheet command object. This needs to go in the master room when complete.
@@ credits: Gattaca at WORA: http://wora.netlosers.com/index.php/topic,3823.0.html

@create DRYH Sheet Commands <DSC>=10
&CMD-+SHEET DRYH Sheet Commands <DSC>=$+sheet:@pemit %#=[u(layout-sheet,%#)];
&CMD-+SHEET_<NAME> DRYH Sheet Commands <DSC>=$+sheet *:@eval setq(E,if(!t(setr(P,pmatch(%0),Player)),Could not find player "%0".)); @switch %qE=,{@pemit %#=[u(layout-sheet,%qP)];},{@pemit %#=[u(prefix)] %qE;}
&LAYOUT-SHEET DRYH Sheet Commands <DSC>=[stdheader([name(%0)]'s Vital Statistic)]%r[columns(Name:[space(3)][get(%0/fullname)]|Discipline:[space(6)][get(%0/_Discipline)]|Gender:[space(1)][get(%0/sex)]|Exhaustion:[space(6)][get(%0/_pool-Exhaustion)]|Age:[space(4)][get(%0/icage)]|Madness:[space(1)][get(%0/_pool-perm_Mad)],37,2,l,0,0,[space(2)],,,1,|,,)]%r[stdsubheader(Responses)]%r[space(2)]Fight:[space(2)][get(%0/_temp-Fight)] remaining out of [get(%0/_perm-Fight)] total.%r[space(2)]Flight:[space(1)][get(%0/_temp-Flight)] remaining out of [get(%0/_perm-Flight)] total.%r[stdsubheader(Abilities)]%r[space(2)]Exhaustion - [before([get(%0/_Exhaustion.ability)],|)]%r[space(2)][after([get(%0/_Exhaustion.ability)],|)]%r%R[space(2)]Madness - [before([get(%0/_Mad.ability)],|)]%r[space(2)][after([get(%0/_Mad.ability)],|)]%r[stdfooter(Use '+bio <name>' to view more character details)]
&CMD_ADD.EXHAUSTION DRYH Sheet Commands <DSC>=$+Exhaustion/add:@switch [gte([get(%#/_pool-Exhaustion)],6)]=1,{@pemit %#=[prefix()] Sorry, you cannot increase your Exhaustion pool above 6, as this would trigger a Distaster. Please use '+disaster/Exhaustion' instead.},0,{&_pool-Exhaustion %#=[add([get(%#/_pool-Exhaustion)],1)];@remit [loc(%#)]=[prefix()] %N has increased %p Exhaustion pool by one.}
&CMD_LOWER.EXHAUSTION DRYH Sheet Commands <DSC>=$+Exhaustion/drop:@switch [lt([get(%#/_pool-Exhaustion)],1)]=1,{@pemit %#=[prefix()] Sorry, you cannot decrease your Exhaustion pool below 0.},0,{&_pool-Exhaustion %#=[sub([get(%#/_pool-Exhaustion)],1)];@remit [loc(%#)]=[prefix()] %N has decreased %p Exhaustion pool by one. This is usually only done when Discipline dominates.}
&CMD_DISASTER.MAD DRYH Sheet Commands <DSC>=$+disaster/Madness:@switch [eq([add([get(%#/_temp-Fight)],[get(%#/_temp-Flight)])],0)][lte([get(%#/_Discipline)],1)]=0*,{@pemit %#=[prefix()] You still have Fight or Flight responses to exhaust. It is not time yet for a Madness Disaster.},11,{@remit [loc(%#)]=[prefix()] %N has submitted to a Madness Disaster and has lost %p last point of Discipline %S will go completely mad or become a monster permanently by the end of the scene. Enjoy!},10,{&_temp-Fight %#=[get(%#/_perm-Fight)];&_temp-Flight %#=[get(%#/_perm-Flight)];&_Discipline %#=[sub([get(%#/_Discipline)],1)];&_pool-perm_Mad %#=[add([get(%#/_pool-perm_Mad)],1)];@remit [loc(%#)]=[prefix()] %N has submitted to a Madness Distaster. %S will temporarily go mad for [add([get(%#/_Discipline)],1)] days. But never fear, %s will get better!;@pemit %#=[prefix()] Your responses have been reset and your Discipline has been lowered to [get(%#/_Discipline)], and you have gained a permanent point of Madness.}
&CMD_DISASTER.EXHAUSTION DRYH Sheet Commands <DSC>=$+disaster/Exhaustion:@switch [gte([get(%#/_pool-Exhaustion)],6)]=0,{@pemit %#=[prefix()] Sorry, your Exhaustion pool is not at 6. There is no need for a Disaster.},1,{&_pool-Exhaustion %#=0;&_temp-Fight %#=[get(%#/_perm-Fight)];&_temp-Flight=[get(%#/_perm-Flight)];@remit [loc(%#)]=[prefix()] %N has submitted to a Exhaustion Disaster!;@pemit %#=[prefix()] Your Exhaustion pool has been reset to zero.},{}
&CMD_FLIGHT.CHECK DRYH Sheet Commands <DSC>=$+check/Flight:@switch [gte([get(%#/_temp-Flight)],1)][gte([get(%#/_temp-Fight)],1)]=01,{@pemit %#=[prefix()] You have exhausted all your Villiany responses already. Please use '+check/Fight' instead.},00,{@pemit %#=[prefix()] You have exhausted all of your Discipline responses! Please use '+disaster/Madness' instead.},1*,{&_temp-Flight %#=[sub([get(%#/_temp-Flight)],1)];@remit [loc(%#)]=[prefix()] %N has checked off a Flight response, bringing %o one step closer to disaster.}
&CMD_FIGHT.CHECK DRYH Sheet Commands <DSC>=$+check/Fight:@switch [gte([get(%#/_temp-Fight)],1)][gte([get(%#/_temp-Flight)],1)]=01,{@pemit %#=[prefix()] You have exhausted all your Fight responses already. Please use '+check/Flight' instead.},00,{@pemit %#=[prefix()] You have exhausted all of your Discipline responses! Please use '+disaster/Madness' instead.},1*,{&_temp-Fight %#=[sub([get(%#/_temp-Fight)],1)];@remit [loc(%#)]=[prefix()] %N has checked off a Fight response, bringing %o one step closer to disaster.}
&CMD_FIGHT.UNCHECK DRYH Sheet Commands <DSC>=$+uncheck/Fight:@switch [lt([get(%#/_temp-Fight)],[get(%#/_perm-Fight)])][lt([get(%#/_temp-Flight)],[get(%#/_perm-Flight)])]=01,{@pemit %#=[prefix()] Your Fight responses are full already. Please use '+uncheck/Flight' instead.},00,{@pemit %#=[prefix()] All of your Discipline responses are full. Use +Exhaustion/drop instead, if you wish.},1*,{&_temp-Fight %#=[add([get(%#/_temp-Fight)],1)];@remit [loc(%#)]=[prefix()] %N has unchecked a Fight response. This is usually only done when Discipline dominates.}
&CMD_FLIGHT.UNCHECK DRYH Sheet Commands <DSC>=$+uncheck/Flight:@switch [lt([get(%#/_temp-Flight)],[get(%#/_perm-Flight)])][lt([get(%#/_temp-Fight)],[get(%#/_perm-Fight)])]=01,{@pemit %#=[prefix()] Your Fight responses are full already. Please use '+uncheck/Fight' instead.},00,{@pemit %#=[prefix()] All of your Discipline responses are full. Use +Exhaustion/drop instead, if you wish.},1*,{&_temp-Flight %#=[add([get(%#/_temp-Flight)],1)];@remit [loc(%#)]=[prefix()] %N has unchecked a Flight response. This is usually only done when Discipline dominates.}
&LAYOUT-BIO DRYH Sheet Commands <DSC>=[stdheader([name(%0)]'s Bio)]%r%r[space(2)]Motivation:%r[space(2)][get(%0/_answer1)]%r%r[space(2)]Origin:%r[space(2)][get(%0/_answer2)]%r%r[space(2)]Purpose:%r[space(2)][get(%0/_answer3)]%r%r[stdsubheader(Titles)]%r[columns([get(%0/_titles)],38,2,l,0,1,[space(2)],,,,|,,)]%r%r[center(%('+' before a title indicates that it has already been spent.%),80,)]%r[stdfooter(Use '+sheet <name>' to view more character details)]
&CMD-+BIO DRYH Sheet Commands <DSC>=$+bio:@pemit %#=[u(layout-bio,%#)];
&CMD-+BIO_<NAME> DRYH Sheet Commands <DSC>=$+bio *:@eval setq(E,if(!t(setr(P,pmatch(%0),Player)),Could not find player "%0".)); @switch %qE=,{@pemit %#=[u(layout-bio,%qP)];},{@pemit %#=[u(prefix)] %qE;}
&CMD_TITLE.REROLL DRYH Sheet Commands <DSC>=$+title/reroll *:@switch [strmatch([get(%#/_titles)],*%0*)][strmatch([get(%#/_titles)],*+%0*)]=0*,{@pemit %#=[prefix()] Sorry, that is not a title you possess. Please check your spelling and try again.},11,{@pemit %#=[prefix()] Sorry, that title has been previously used for a re-roll. Please try again.},10,{&_titles %#=[edit([get(%#/_titles)],%0,+%0)];@remit [loc(%#)]=[prefix()] %N expends %o Title - "%0" to call for a reroll of the conflict. This is a reroll of any one player's (or the GM's) entire pool.}
&CMD_TITLE.DISCIPLINE DRYH Sheet Commands <DSC>=$+title/Discipline *:@switch [strmatch([get(%#/_titles)],*%0*)][strmatch([get(%#/_titles)],*+%0*)][lt([get(%#/_Discipline)],3)]=0**,{@pemit %#=[prefix()] Sorry, that is not a title you possess. Please check your spelling and try again.},111,{@pemit %#=[prefix()] Sorry, that title has been previously used for a re-roll. Please try again.},100,{@pemit %#=[prefix()] Sorry, your Discipline pool is at the maximum of 3.,101,{&_titles %#=[edit([get(%#/_titles)],%0,+%0)];&_Discipline %#=[add([get(%#/_Discipline)],1)];@pemit %#=[prefix()] You expend your Title - "%0" to increase your Discipline to [get(%#/_Discipline)].}
&CMD_RESET.EXHAUSTION DRYH Sheet Commands <DSC>=$+Exhaustion/reset:&_pool-Exhaustion %#=0;@remit [loc(%#)]=[prefix()] %N has reset %p Exhaustion pool to Zero. This should only be done between story episodes.
&CMD_TITLE/GIVE DRYH Sheet Commands <DSC>=$+title/give *:@switch [gt(words(setq(p,[lcon(loc(%#)/connect)]),1))]=0,{@pemit %#=[prefix()] You cannot give out a title to only yourself.},1,{@dolist %qp=@edit ##/_titles=$,|%0;@remit loc(%#)=[prefix()] The occupants of this room have been awarded a Title by %N for participating in an adventure.;@trigger #177/trig_create=%#,#373,0,Title Awarded,[eval(%N has awarded a title to the following folks at [time()]: [iter(%qp,[name(##)])]%r%rTitle Awarded: %0.}
&credits DRYH Sheet Commands <DSC>=DRYH Dice Code conceived and started by Cobalt and Thenomain. Completed and expanded by Gattaca. DRYH +sheet and cgen by Gattaca. Use, alter, and distribute at will.
@rxlevel DRYH Sheet Commands <DSC>=Real
@txlevel DRYH Sheet Commands <DSC>=Real
@set DRYH Sheet Commands <DSC>=INHERIT

@@Creates the DRYH Dice Object <DDO>
@pemit me=Creating the Dice Roller. This needs to go in the master room once complete.
@create DRYH Dice Roller <DDO>=10
&DISCIPLINE DRYH Dice Roller <DDO>=3
&EXHAUSTION DRYH Dice Roller <DDO>=2
&MADNESS DRYH Dice Roller <DDO>=2
&FIL.3 DRYH Dice Roller <DDO>=lte(%0, 3)
&PROTO.ROLLER DRYH Dice Roller <DDO>=$+dryh:@remit [loc(%#)]=[prefix()] %N rolls:%r[ansi(h, Discipline)][space(5)][ansi(hx, Exhaustion)][space(5)][ansi(hg, ljust(Madness, 12))]%r[setr(0, ansi(h, ljust(setr(d, lrand(1, 6, u(%#/_Discipline))), 12), hx, ljust(setr(e, lrand(1, 6, u(%#/_pool-Exhaustion))), 12), hg, ljust(setr(m, lrand(1, 6, u(%#/_pool-perm_Mad))), 12)))][ansi(h, Degree)]: [words(filter(ddo/fil.3, [stripansi(%q0)]))], [ansi(h, Strongest)]: [case(rest(u(compare-rolls, %qd, %qe, %qm), :), d, Discipline, e, Exhaustion, m, Madness, ???)]
&COMPARE-ROLL-PREPARE DRYH Dice Roller <DDO>=[@@(%0 - list of dice 5 3 1, %1 - # of most dice used this roll)][ljust(edit(revwords(sort(%0)), %b, ), %1, 0)]
&COMPARE-ROLLS DRYH Dice Roller <DDO>=[@@(%0 - Discipline rolls, %1 - Exhaustion, %2 - Madness %3 - pain, not all are necessary)][setq(l, max(words(%0), words(%1), words(%2), words(%3)))][last(sort([u(compare-roll-prepare, %0, %ql)]:d [u(compare-roll-prepare, %1, %ql)]:e [u(compare-roll-prepare, %2, %ql)]:m))]
&CMD_PLUS.ROLL DRYH Dice Roller <DDO>=$+roll:@remit [loc(%#)]=[stdsubheader(%N rolls!)]%r[space(3)][ansi(h, Discipline)][space(5)][ansi(hx, Exhaustion)][space(5)][ansi(hg, ljust(Madness, 12))]%r[space(3)][setr(0, ansi(h, ljust(setr(d, lrand(1, 6, u(%#/_Discipline))), 12), hx, ljust(setr(e, lrand(1, 6, u(%#/_pool-Exhaustion))), 12), hg, ljust(setr(m, lrand(1, 6, u(%#/_pool-perm_Mad))), 16)))][ansi(h, Degree of Success)]: [words(filter(ddo/fil.3, [stripansi(%q0)]))]%r[stdfooter(Strongest is [case(rest(u(compare-rolls, %qd, %qe, %qm), :), d,[ansi(h,Discipline)] at [lmax(%qd)], e,[ansi(hx,Exhaustion)] at [lmax(%qe)], m,[ansi(hg,Madness)] at [lmax(%qm)], ???)])]
&CMD_PLUS.ROLL.MAD DRYH Dice Roller <DDO>=$+roll/mad *:@remit [loc(%#)]=[stdsubheader(%N rolls with %ch%cg%0%cn added Madness)]%r[space(3)][ansi(h, Discipline)][space(5)][ansi(hx, Exhaustion)][space(5)][ansi(hg, ljust(Madness, 12))]%r[space(3)][setr(0, ansi(h, ljust(setr(d, lrand(1, 6, u(%#/_Discipline))), 12), hx, ljust(setr(e, lrand(1, 6, u(%#/_pool-Exhaustion))), 12), hg, ljust(setr(m, lrand(1, 6, [add([u(%#/_pool-perm_Mad)],%0)])), 16)))][ansi(h, Degree of Success)]: [words(filter(ddo/fil.3, [stripansi(%q0)]))]%r[stdfooter(Strongest is [case(rest(u(compare-rolls, %qd, %qe, %qm), :), d,[ansi(h,Discipline)] at [lmax(%qd)], e,[ansi(hx,Exhaustion)] at [lmax(%qe)], m,[ansi(hg,Madness)] at [lmax(%qm)], ???)])]
&CMD_PLUS.PAIN DRYH Dice Roller <DDO>=$+roll/pain *:@remit [loc(%#)]=[stdsubheader(%N rolls %0 Pain!)]%r[space(3)][ansi(hr, Pain!)]%r[space(3)][setr(0, ansi(hr, ljust(setr(x, lrand(1, 6, %0)), 40)))][ansi(h, Degree of Success)]: [words(filter(ddo/fil.3, [stripansi(%q0)]))]%r[stdfooter(Strongest is [ansi(hr,Pain)] at [lmax([stripansi(%q0)])])]
&credits DRYH Dice Roller <DDO>=DRYH roller code conceived and started by Cobalt and Thenomain. Completed and expanded by Gattaca. DRYH +sheet and cgen by Gattaca. Use, alter, and distribute at will.
@rxlevel DRYH Dice Roller <DDO>=Real
@txlevel DRYH Dice Roller <DDO>=Real
@set DRYH Dice Roller <DDO>=INHERIT

@pemit me=Setting up Character Creation rooms and approval code.
@dig/teleport Character Creation=Character Creation <CC>;cc
@Desc here=%rWelcome to our very brief and very easy character creation!%rIn this first room you will answer a few background questions and set up the basics of your character. In the next, you will set your three stats. If you need any help, please contact a staffer for assistance.%r%r* First, set your full name. [ansi(h,&fullname me=<name>)]%r* Then set your IC age. [ansi(h,&icage me=<age>)]%r* Finally, set the Title of your first adventure. [ansi(h,+title me=<Title>)]%r%r%rNow, please provide brief (one paragraph) answers to the following questions.%r%r%t1. Where do you come from?%r%t2. What do you know about the end of the world?%r%t3. What is you secret?%r%t* Type [ansi(h,+answer <#>=<info>)] to store your answers.%r%rYou should also take a moment to do the following:%r* @desc me=<description>%r* @sex me=<Male|Female|Neuter>%r* @lock me=me%r%rWhen you are finished - type '%chOnward%cn'%r[stdfooter()]
@Sfail here=[prefix()] This room has been set silent - please use the new player channel to request help.
&ISSTAFF here=[isstaff(%#)]
@lock/SpeechLock here=ISSTAFF/1
&CMD-ANSWERS here=$+answer *=*:@switch [between(1,5,%0,1)]=1,{&_answer%0 %#=%1;@pemit %#=[prefix()] You have set you answer to question %0 as: %1.},0,{@pemit %#=[prefix()] Sorry, you must choose a question number 1 to 5 to answer.}
&CMD-1ST_TITLE here=$+title me=*:&_titles %#=%0;@pemit %#=[prefix()] You have set your first Title to: %0
@NameFormat here=[stdsubheader(name(me))]
@rxlevel here=Real
@txlevel here=Real
@set here=AUDITORIUM
@set here=SAFE
@set here=INHERIT
@parent here=OOC Zone

@dig/teleport Character Creation – Stats=Onward,Go Back
@Desc here=In this room you will set your stats. There are only three, so do not panic!%R%rFirst set your Discipline responses. You get three total responses to assign between Fight and Flight, and you can set these however you like. Remember that a Flight response does not nessicarily make you a bad person.%r%r* Type [ansi(h,+choose Fight)] or [ansi(h,+choose Flight)] to set each response.%r%r%rNow explain your Exhaunstion Ability. This is something normal that you can do exceptionally well. Such as performing complex calculations as fast a computer, knowing exactly where to look for a arcane book in the library, or shooting a gun with impossible accuracy.%r%r* Type %ch+Exhaustion <name of ability>=<brief explanation of ability>%cn%r%r%rFinally, explain your Madness Ability. This is some minor abnormal, supernatural, or cult ability (or connection) that you have. Maybe you get a tingle when the unnatural is around, maybe you have prophetic nightmares, maybe you can do ancient Eqyptian Magic, maybe you can read minds, or become overlooked.%r%r* Type %ch+Madness <name of ability>=<brief explanation of ability>%cn%r%rThat is it! You can check out your character sheet by typing [ansi(h,+sheet)] and then type '%chOnward%cn' when you are ready to enter play.%R[stdfooter()]
&CMD-EXHAUSTION here=$+Exhaustion *=*:&_exhaustion.ability %#=%0|%1;@pemit %#=[prefix()]You have set you Exhaustion ability to: %0, with an explanation of: %1.
&CMD-MAD here=$+Madness *=*:&_mad.ability %#=%0|%1;@pemit %#=[prefix()]You have set you Madness ability to: %0, with an explanation of: %1.
&CMD-FIGHT here=$+choose fight:@switch [gte([get(%#/_respleft)],1)]=1,{&_perm-fight %#=[add([get(%#/_perm-fight)],1)];&_temp-fight %#=[add([get(%#/_temp-fight)],1)];&_respleft %#=[sub([get(%#/_respleft)],1)];@pemit %#=[prefix()] You have assigned one response to Fight. You have [ansi(hy,[get(%#/_respleft)])] remaining to assign.},0,{@pemit %#=[prefix()] All of your responses have already been spent.}
&CMD-FLIGHT here=$+choose flight:@switch [gte([get(%#/_respleft)],1)]=1,{&_perm-flight %#=[add([get(%#/_perm-flight)],1)];&_temp-flight %#=[add([get(%#/_temp-flight)],1)];&_respleft %#=[sub([get(%#/_respleft)],1)];@pemit %#=[prefix()] You have assigned one response to Flight. You have [ansi(hy,[get(%#/_respleft)])] remaining to assign.},0,{@pemit %#=[prefix()] All of your responses have already been spent.}
&SFAIL($) here=[prefix()] This room has been set silent - please use the new player channel to request help.
&ISSTAFF here=[isstaff(%#)]
@lock/SpeechLock here=ISSTAFF/1
&CMD_ONWARD here=$onward:@wipe %#/_cg-errors;@switch %qE=,{@pemit %#=%R; @dolist lattrp(me/check-*)={@pemit %#=setr(E,ulocal(me/##,%#));&_cg-errors %#=add(default(%#/_cg-errors,0),words(trim(squish(%qE,|),b,|),|));};@wait 1={@pemit %#=[prefix()] Check complete. Found [setr(T,default(%#/_cg-errors,0))] issue[if(neq(%qT,1),s)].;@switch gte(%qT,1)=1,{@pemit %#=[prefix()] You have not set everything required by character creation.;},{@pemit %#=[prefix()] Onward to your Doom!;@link %#=#978;&_cgcomplete %#=[time()];@set %#=APPROVED;@tel %#=#0; @trigger #177/trig_create=%#,#206,0,[eval(Cgen Complete %N)],[eval(%N has completed cgen and entered play. Review at leisure for code bugs or glaring errors. Only Cgen Staff should complete this +job.)]};}},{@pemit %#=u(prefix) %qE;}
&CHECK-ANSWER1 here=[if(!hasattr(%#,_answer1),%ch%crRequired:%cn You need to provide an answer to Question 1.)]
&CHECK-ANSWER2 here=[if(!hasattr(%#,_answer2),%ch%crRequired:%cn You need to provide an answer to Question 2.)]
&CHECK-ANSWER3 here=[if(!hasattr(%#,_answer3),%ch%crRequired:%cn You need to provide an answer to Question 3.)]
&CHECK-RESP here=[if(gte([get(%#/_respleft)],1),%ch%crRequired:%cn You need to assign all of your Fight/Flight responses.)]
&CHECK-MAD here=[if(not(strmatch(get(%0/_mad.ability),*|*)),%ch%crRequired:%cn You need to set your Madness Ability.)]
&CHECK-EXHAUSTION here=[if(not(strmatch(get(%0/_exhaustion.ability),*|*)),%ch%crRequired:%cn You need to set your Exhaustion Ability.)]
&CHECK-DESC here=[if(!hasattr(%#,desc),%ch%crRequired:%cn You need to set your Description with @desc me=<desc>.)]
&CHECK-TITLE here=[if(!hasattr(%#,_titles),%ch%crRequired:%cn You need to provide your first Title.)]
&CHECK-FULLNAME here=[if(!hasattr(%#,fullname),%ch%crRequired:%cn You need to provide your character's full name.)]
@rxlevel here=Real
@txlevel here=Real
@set here=SAFE
@set here=INHERIT
@pemit me=All done! Enjoy!
