Gittles the Sarlona Cleric

Gittles is my cleric on the Sarlona server. I have my own guild/own guild ship on Sarlona, and a few semi-geared toons (including another Eachna, and a bard named Galleya).

  • Back to Tips For DDO Noobs - My DDO Toons

Gittles's TR Record

After returning to DDO in 2019, I decided to TR Gittles. I needed to do some sort of reincarnation on her because she didn't have a domain. She was level 21, so grinding up to 30 would have taken me a while. My healing bard is level 27, so I still have a toon I can take to raids. If I was going to have to reset everything anyway, I decided that starting Gittles back at level 1 and getting a cleric past life was the best plan.

I took her through Korthos and ate some xp stones from daily dice rolls, she's at level 3.

I'm not really sure what I want to do with her. I'm thinking this life she may mostly be a turning cleric. I took the Death domain to see how it goes. In heroic levels turning shouldn't be too bad. She has a few pieces of old heroic turning gear in her bank.

I plan to use this as a test run for doing cleric lives on Orien. I can sort out whatever problems I run into and make sure things go more smoothly on Orien.
