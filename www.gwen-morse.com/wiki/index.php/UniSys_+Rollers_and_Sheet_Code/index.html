UniSys +Rollers and Sheet Code

From Gwen Morse's Wiki

These snippets were ripped brutally from a thread over on WORA, and are a fine example of why it helps to have completed systems :D. This isn't a completed system, it's not even all the functions you need to make the posted examples work.

Still, if you're starting from *nothing*, even little snippets may be helpful.


Whirlwind's Unisys Roller: 


CMD-UNIROLL: $+roll *:@remit %l=GAME> %n +rolls a [setq(R, rand(1, 10))][setr(0, u(FN-UNIRECURSION, %qR, %qR, %qR))] plus [parse(secure(%0), capstr(lcstr(##)),,+)] [setq(1, ladd(setr(2, parse(secure(%0), if(isnum(##), floor(##), [max(attr-get(%#, ##), skill-get(%#, ##))])))))]<[parse(%q2,##,,+)][if(abs(setr(L, ladd(u(#46/FN_LUCK-CHECK, %#)))), +%qL Luck,)]>: [setr(3, add(%q1, %q0, %qL))] <[switch(%q3, <9, No, <11, 1st, <13, 2nd, <15, 3rd, <17, 4th, <21, 5th, [sub(div(%q3, 3), 1)]th)] Lvl Succ>

FN-UNIRECURSION: [@@(%0 = pending die. %1 = current total. %2 = upping or downing)][setq(U, rand(1, 10))][case(%0-%2, 1-1, u(FN-UNIRECURSION, %qU, [add(%1, min(sub(%qU, 5), 0))], %2), 10-10, u(FN-UNIRECURSION, %qU, [add(%1, max(sub(%qU, 5), 0))], %2), %1)]

Cobolt's +roll snippets 

Baseroller stuff:
think [setr(1, u(#18/fn.roller))] - [switch(words(%q1), 1, %q1, 2, [u(#18/fn.rule_of_checker, %q1)], 3, [u(#18/fn.multiple_rule_of_checker, %q1)])]

The many functions I'm making to make it work so far:
FN.OUTCOME_LEVEL: [switch(%0, >23, Mind-boggling, >20, Extraordinary, >16, Excellent, >14, Very Good, >12, Good, >10, Decent, >8, Adequate, Failure)]
FN.ROLLER: [switch(setr(0, lrand(1, 10, 1)), 1, %q0 [u(#18/fn.roller)], 10, %q0 [u(#18/fn.roller)], %q0)]
FN.RULE_OF_CHECKER: switch(first(%0), 10, [u(#18/fn.rule_of_ten, [rest(%0)])], 1, [u(#18/fn.rule_of_one, [rest(%0)])])
FN.RULE_OF_TEN: [switch(%0, >9, 15, >8, 14, >7, 13, >6, 12, >5, 11, 10)]
FN.RULE_OF_ONE: [switch(%0, >4, 1, >3, -1, >2, -2, >1, -3)]

Glitch's +roll contribution: 


F.ROLL: [setq(r, rand(1, 10))][if(setinter(%qr, 1 10), u(%!/f.reroll))]%qr
F.REROLL: [setq(t, rand(1, 10))][if(gt(%qr, 1), setq(r, add(%qr, max(0, sub(%qt, 5)))), if(eq(%qt, 1), setq(r, min(-5, add(%qr, -5))), if(lt(%qt, 5), setq(r, add(%qr, sub(%qt, 5))))))][if(setinter(%qt, 1 10), u(%!/f.reroll))]


Cobolt's Starting Sheet code: 


C.+SHEET_SELF: $+sheet:@pemit %#=[header(name(%#)'s +sheet)]%r[ljust(ansi(h, Name:), 9)][get(%#/fullname)][space(5)][ansi(h, Experience:)]%b[edit(get(%#/_xp), |, %/)][space(5)][ansi(h, Channeling Level:)]%b[get(%#/_channeling_level)]%r[ljust(ansi(h, Concept:), 9)][get(%#/concept)]%r[divider(Attributes)]%r[ljust(ansi(h, Strength:), 14)][get(%#/_attribute.strength)][space(5)][ljust(ansi(h,Intelligence:), 14)][get(%#/_attribute.intelligence)][space(5)][ljust(ansi(h, Life:), 7)][add(mul(add([get(%#/_attribute.Constitution)], [get(%#/_attribute.Strength)]), 4), 10)][space(5)][ljust(ansi(h, Endurance:), 12)][add(mul(add([get(%#/_attribute.Constitution)], [get(%#/_attribute.Strength)], [get(%#/_attribute.willpower)]), 3), 5)]%r[ljust(ansi(h, Dexterity:), 14)][get(%#/_attribute.dexterity)][space(5)][ljust(ansi(h, Perception:), 14)][get(%#/_attribute.perception)][space(5)][ljust(ansi(h, Speed:), 7)][mul(add([get(%#/_attribute.constitution)], [get(%#/_attribute.dexterity)]), 2)][space(5)][ljust(ansi(h, Essence:), 12)][u(#23/fn.essence_total, %#)]%r[ljust(ansi(h, Constitution:), 14)][get(%#/_attribute.constitution)][space(5)][ljust(ansi(h, Willpower:),14)][get(%#/_attribute.willpower)]%r[divider(Qualities & Drawbacks)]%r[u(#23/fn.qualities_drawbacks, %#)][divider(Skills)]%r[u(#23/fn.skills, %#)][divider(Powers)]%r[u(#23/fn.powers, %#)][footer()]

FN.ESSENCE_TOTAL: ladd(iter(lattr(%0/_attribute.*), get(%0/%i0)))

FN.QUALITIES_DRAWBACKS: [columns(iter([lattr(%0/_quality.*)] [lattr(%0/_drawback.*)], [ljust(ansi(h, rest(%i0, .)):, 17)][get(%0/%i0)], , |), 39, |)]

FN.SKILLS: [columns(iter([lattr(%0/_skill.*)], [ljust(ansi(h, rest(%i0, .)):, 17)][get(%0/%i0)], , |), 39, |)]

FN.POWERS: [columns(iter([lattr(%0/_power.*)], [ljust(ansi(h, edit(rest(%i0, .), _, %b)):, 18)][get(%0/%i0)], , |), 39, |)]

CALC_LIFE_POINTS: [ladd(0 [mul([stat.get(%0,QUALITIES,Hard To Kill)],3)] [mul([ladd([u(calc_strength,%0)] [u(calc_constitution,%0)])],4)] 10  )]

CALC_ENDURANCE_POINTS: [setq(v,[ladd(0 [mul([stat.get(%0,Qualities,Tireless)],9)] [mul([ladd([u(calc_strength,%0)] [u(calc_willpower,%0)] [u(calc_constitution,%0)])],3)] 5 )])][switch(1,[compstat(%0,DRAWBACKs,Physical Disability/Overweight,2)],[div(%qv,2)],[compstat(%0,drawbacks,physical disability/overweight,1)],[sub(%qv,[div(%qv,4)])],%qv)]

CALC_ESSENCE_POOL: [ladd(0 [mul(5,[stat.get(%0,QUALITIES,Increased Essence pool)])] [u(calc_artistic_talent,%0)] [iter(Willpower Constitution Strength Intelligence Perception Dexterity,[u(calc_##,%0)],%B,%B)] )]
