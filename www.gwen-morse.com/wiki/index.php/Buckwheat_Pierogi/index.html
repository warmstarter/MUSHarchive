Buckwheat pierogi

  • 1 cup buckwheat
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 Tablespoon melted butter
  • 1-2 Tablespoon water

Filling: already cooked meat and/or vegetables

Mix all of the ingredients in order except the filling, adding water while you stir until the consistency is like bread dough. On wax paper or a pastry mat roll out the dough into an oval making sure that the center is thicker than the edges. Place a spoonful or so of the filling in the center of the dough. Holding the wax paper with the dough in your hands fold the ends of the dough around the meat. Pinch the edges of the pierogi so that it seals around the meat. In a pan, melt some butter to prevent sticking, then place the pierogi on one side over low heat. After about 10 minutes gently flip the pierogi over to cook the other side. Once it is done set on a plate to cool a little bit before eating.

Comments: Use a coffee grinder or bullet blender to grind buckwheat groats
