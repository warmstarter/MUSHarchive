Cult of Ecstasy

Here is a sample calling for the Cult of Ecstasy. Ecstatics are drug-using transcendentalists.

INT: 0 PER: +2
WIL: +2 CHA: +1
STR: -1 DEX: +1
CON: 0 SPD: 0

Skills

  • Lore (Pharmacology): +6
  • Seduction: +6
  • Perform (any one style): +6
  • Concentration: +5
  • Lore (Occult) +3 OR Lore (Cosmology): +3
  • Evade: +3

+6 to distribute between the True Magick skills and/or to add points to existing skills.

One Additional non-Sphere Talent Tree, with two talents.
