Simple Globals

A series of globals, including +pronounce (how to pronounce someone's name, given they've set an attribute), +laston (last time someone was on), +where, +inspect (room inspector for missing attributes and such), and +globals (list of globals).

Author: Maxi Rose
Category: Globals
Commands: @force, @pemit, @switch, @trigger.
Functions: abs(), ceil(), center(), controls(), convtime(), div(), edefault(), edit(), extract(), fdiv(), flags(), fullname(), get(), gt(), gte(), hasflag(), isdbref(), iter(), lattr(), lcon(), lexits(), ljust(), lnum(), loc(), locate(), lock(), lte(), match(), matchall(), member(), mid(), mul(), name(), num(), or(), owner(), parse(), r(), repeat(), rjust(), round(), secs(), setinter(), setq(), setunion(), sort(), space(), strlen(), strmatch(), sub(), switch(), trim(), trunc(), u(), v(), wordpos(), words().
Compatibility: CobraMUSH, PennMUSH, TinyMUX.

Pronounce command: Lets people write the phonetics of how to pronounce
their character's name. Nothing more than an attr call.
LastOn command: Will tell you when a specified player last logged on, and
how long they were on for.
NewLast command: An update to LastOn, I think.
Where command: Gives a columned list of all the players and where they are.
Inspect command: Helps make sure all rooms and their exits have appropriate
descs and osucc, etc. Works one room at a time.
Globals command: I think it tells all staff what globals are in the master
room along with any players that may be there.
