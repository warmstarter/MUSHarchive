Roulette

A roulette wheel with ANSI graphics. (This keno game uses a Role Playing MUSH money system, and the &VAR_MONEY has to be changed accordingly. With a little work it could be made to work using the built-in MUSH currency.)

Author: Demonic
Category: Games
Commands: @aenter, @create, @describe, @enter, @failure, @idescribe, @leave, @oenter, @oleave, @oxenter, @oxleave, @parent, @set, @switch, @trigger, @wait.
Functions: abs(), add(), after(), and(), ansi(), center(), dec(), default(), div(), edit(), eq(), extract(), first(), grab(), gt(), gte(), hasflag(), hastype(), ifelse(), inc(), isdbref(), iter(), last(), ldelete(), left(), ljust(), loc(), lock(), lte(), match(), member(), mid(), mod(), mul(), name(), not(), num(), pemit(), poss(), rand(), remit(), repeat(), rjust(), secure(), set(), setq(), shuffle(), sort(), space(), sub(), switch(), trunc(), u(), ucstr(), udefault(), v(), words(), xget().
Compatibility: CobraMUSH, PennMUSH, TinyBit, TinyMUX.
