Astrogator

The Astrogator was a device used for a while on Otherspace as a navigation aid when flying ships in Hspace (2 and/or 3). It was necessary in a game where ships could use the standard Hspace hyperdrive and distant stars often could not be seen on sensors until one was very close.

Author: Dahan
Category: Dynamic Space
Commands: @aclone, @cpattr, @create, @describe, @lastpaged, @link, @parent, @pemit, @set, @switch.
Functions: abs(), add(), and(), ansi(), capstr(), center(), comp(), dec(), dist3d(), div(), edit(), extract(), fdiv(), first(), grab(), graball(), gt(), gte(), haspower(), if(), ifelse(), index(), isnum(), iter(), lattr(), lcstr(), ljust(), locate(), lt(), match(), mid(), mod(), mul(), name(), parent(), power(), repeat(), rest(), rjust(), room(), round(), secure(), set(), setdiff(), setq(), setunion(), sign(), sort(), sortby(), space(), sqrt(), squish(), sub(), switch(), t(), time(), timestring(), type(), u(), v(), wipe(), words(), x().
