MUDlike Socials

A large collection of social commands, similar to the social commands used on hack and slash MUDs.

Author: Cheetah@M*U*S*H
Category: Other
Commands: @create, @lset, @set, @trigger, @wa.
Functions: ansi(), case(), edit(), elements(), first(), if(), ifelse(), lattr(), lcstr(), loc(), match(), name(), not(), obj(), oemit(), or(), pemit(), poss(), regeditall(), regediti(), rest(), rnum(), setq(), setr(), strmatch(), subj(), switch(), t(), table(), u(), v().
Compatibility: PennMUSH.

Instructions

Copy and paste the below code into a compatible MUSH or MUX.
