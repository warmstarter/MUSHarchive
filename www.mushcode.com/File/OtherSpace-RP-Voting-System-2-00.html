OtherSpace RP Voting System 2.00

The voting system is intended to ensure that outstanding roleplaying is commended, and poor roleplaying pointed out and improved. Comments should be of substance. Only administrators and directors can view who you voted for. At the end of a voting cycle, your comments (but not the author) are visible to the person you commented on.

Author: OtherSpace
Category: Globals
Commands: @create, @dolist, @force, @link, @mail, @mailcurf, @pemit, @power, @select, @set, @teleport, @wipe.
Functions: add(), and(), ansi(), cemit(), comp(), dec(), edit(), elements(), escape(), first(), get(), grab(), gt(), hasattr(), hasflag(), haspower(), if(), ifelse(), iter(), lattr(), ldelete(), lit(), ljust(), match(), mod(), name(), not(), num(), or(), orflags(), pemit(), pmatch(), repeat(), replace(), rest(), secure(), set(), setdiff(), setq(), setunion(), sortby(), squish(), strlen(), sub(), t(), time(), u(), ulocal(), v(), words().
